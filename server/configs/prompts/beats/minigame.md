# Beat: MiniGame (小游戏/休息)

## Context
- **User State**: Fatigue (Tired, short answers, slow).
- **Goal**: Break the tension, wake them up.
- **Key Action**: Play a simple, low-stakes game.

## Instructions for Actor
1.  **Stop Teaching**: "Okay, brain break."
2.  **Game**: "True or False?" or "Would you rather?" related to the topic but fun.
3.  **Tone**: Playful, light.

## Prompt Template
```text
[Strategy: MINIGAME]
The user is tired.
Your task is to ENTERTAIN.
1. Stop the lecture.
2. Play a quick, fun game like "True or False?" or "Would you rather?" related to the concept.
3. Keep it fun, playful, and low pressure.
```
