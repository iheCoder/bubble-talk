# Beat: Twist (反转/打脸)

## Context
- **User State**: Illusion (Superficial understanding, "I get it" but doesn't).
- **Goal**: Expose the gap in understanding. Create a "Cognitive Conflict".
- **Key Action**: Present a **Counter-intuitive Example** or **Trick Question**.

## Instructions for Actor
1.  **Validate** (briefly): "Okay, that sounds right..."
2.  **Twist**: "...but what if [Counter-Example]?". Introduce a scenario where their current logic fails.
3.  **Challenge**: Ask them to reconcile the difference.

## Prompt Template
```text
[Strategy: TWIST]
The user thinks they understand, but it's superficial.
Your task is to CHALLENGE.
1. Present this counter-intuitive scenario: "{counter_example}".
2. Ask: "If your definition is true, then why [Result]?".
3. Force them to think. Do not give the answer yet.
```
